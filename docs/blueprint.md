# **App Name**: Roemah Jumpoetan Palembang

## Core Features:

- Homepage Display: Display of premium Jumputan products with a focus on high-quality visuals and brand storytelling.
- Product Catalog: Display categorized Jumputan products (Kain, Pakaian, Aksesoris) with detailed product information, images, and pricing.
- Shopping Cart Management: Enable users to add products to a shopping cart, review items, and proceed to checkout.
- User Account Management: Allow users to create and manage accounts for order tracking and personalized experiences.
- Product Search and Filtering: Enable users to search for products by keywords and filter by category, price, and other attributes.
- Customer Testimonials: Display customer testimonials to build trust and credibility.
- Newsletter Subscription: Allow users to subscribe to a newsletter for updates on new collections and promotions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy brown (#A67B5B) to evoke a sense of warmth and authenticity.
- Background color: Soft cream (#F5F5DC) for a clean and elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Indigo blue (#4B0082) as a complementary color that references traditional dyes. Use on CTAs and highlights.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif font, to convey elegance.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font, for readability.
- Use simple, elegant line icons that reflect the brand's focus on quality and craftsmanship.
- Incorporate ample white space and split layouts to achieve a clean and modern aesthetic.
